Found a IBM Selectric II typewriter in the trash and decided to convert it 
to connect to a computer. Managed to take a whole bunch of pictures and 
short videos, so I had to make a full video about it.

Link to 3D files and firmware:
- https://github.com/alnwlsn/videos/tree/main/selectric
